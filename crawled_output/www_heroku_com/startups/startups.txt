Heroku AI PaaS for Startups: Scale Your Business QuicklyRun business-critical apps on a fully managed platform.Get Started Now
Fast track your startup businessHeroku is a cloud-based, AI platform-as-a-service (AI PaaS) that gives startup developers a powerful solution for building, deploying, and operating applications. The platform is fully managed, meaning that we take care of servers, hardware, and infrastructure, so you can keep your team lean and focused on building a great product. As business grows, Heroku is there for you over the long-term, with seamless scalability and advanced features to support your app success.Get your great ideas to market quickly
Build apps your wayThe platform’s flexibility gives startup developers the freedom of choice. Use your favorite tools, languages, databases, and services that work best for your app, team, and business.
Extend your app in a few clicks
Our ecosystem includes 200+ fully managed Heroku Add-on services that support every stage of app development, such as monitoring, logging, messaging, or search.
Ship apps fast and often
Heroku helps startup developers stay highly productive. Deploy directly from popular tools like Git, GitHub, or Docker, and use our easy-to-use structured CI/CD workflow.
Work with data easily
Heroku’s managed data services are optimized for developers, making it even easier to build data-driven apps and use data to make better decisions, faster.
Simplify your DevOps
Heroku is a great platform for startups. We monitor uptime, performance, and infrastructure, so you can avoid hiring dedicated staff. Anyone on your team can manage app operations from a shared dashboard.
Grow your business on one robust platform
Experiment & prototype
Try new things easily on Heroku before turning them into product. A range of low-cost services can help your business keep R&D costs affordable.
Launch new features
Respond quickly to market changes and customer needs by quickly iterating your apps. Deploy updates on every push with Heroku’s GitHub integration.
Build & manage your team
Heroku’s ease of use makes onboarding new hires a breeze. Heroku Teams helps you manage your users, apps, and billing as your team grows.
Scale seamlessly into the future
As demand grows, scale up your dynos instantly, or move to a dedicated runtime environment for greater privacy, power, and performance.
Meet enterprise-grade requirements
Simplify HIPAA & PCI compliance
Heroku Shield makes it easier to build apps for regulated industries, such as healthcare or financial services.
Run high traffic, demanding apps
Not just for large companies, Heroku Enterprise offers additional services for apps that need network isolation, dedicated resources, and greater control.
Build customer-centric apps with Salesforce data
Heroku Connect syncs data seamlessly between Heroku Postgres and Salesforce, so you can do more with your CRM data.
