About BuzzFeed - Website and Technology Accessibility StatementAboutPressJobsContactInvestorsLegalWEBSITE AND TECHNOLOGY ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENTBuzzFeed values audience diversity and inclusion, and to that end, we consistently work to further enhance the accessibility of our websites to visitors who use digital assistive technologies like screen readers, screen magnifiers, or voice recognition software. We view accessibility as an ongoing effort.We are:Maintaining measures that meet Levels A and AA of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) version 2.0;Engaging with accessibility and usability specialists to advise us on our efforts and provide regular third-party testing of our websites; andReviewing existing measures for potential improvements in performance, speed, and accessibility.If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, please contact support@buzzfeed.com.
