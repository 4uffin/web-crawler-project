An overnight freeze is ideal if you have the time, but you can shorten the wait to about 2 hours by freezing the juices in a wide shallow dish to maximize the surface area. Because it’s such a simple mixture of fruit juice, tequila, and sugar, opting for freshly squeezed goes a long way here.Want more frozen cocktail recipes?
