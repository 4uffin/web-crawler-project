The saltiness feels restorative while the spicy kick jolts me awake long enough to ease back into a nice little buzz. As the years have gone by, I’ve been perfecting my recipe—tweaking it just so to get the right amount of savory umami tartness. The kimchi brine is the secret hero here; just a splash of it brightens the cocktail while deepening it with a little funky je ne sais quoi. Don’t believe me?
