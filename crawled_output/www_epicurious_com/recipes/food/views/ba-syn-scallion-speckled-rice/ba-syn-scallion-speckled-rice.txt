Think tiny ingredient lists, laid-back techniques, and results so delicious you’ll text home about them.Scallion-infused oil, or pa gireum in Korean, is a fragrant way to start a skillet rice dish. If you prefer to cook rice in a rice cooker (we don’t blame you), sizzle the scallions in the oil as directed then transfer the scallion oil to a rice cooker. Add rice and the corresponding amount of water as marked on your rice cooker.
