A BCG Global Survey of 1,000 CXOs in more than 20 sectors reports that just 26% of organizations have achieved value from AI, realizing an average cost savings of 45% and 60% higher revenue growth compared to peer firms.Karim R. Lakhani
is the Dorothy and Michael Hintze Professor of Business Administration at Harvard Business School and the chair and a cofounder of the Digital Data Design Institute at Harvard University. He is a coauthor of Competing in the Age of AI (Harvard Business Review Press, 2020).JSJen Stave
