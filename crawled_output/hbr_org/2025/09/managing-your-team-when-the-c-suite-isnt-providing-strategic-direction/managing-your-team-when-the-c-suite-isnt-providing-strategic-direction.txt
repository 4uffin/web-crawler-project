As leadership froze, accountability rolled downhill, leaving Lauren to manage confusion, stalled progress, and a restless team.Jenny Fernandez , MBA, is a corporate leadership consultant and executive coach who partners with senior leaders and their teams to become more adaptive, effective, and resilient. She is a faculty member at Columbia University and NYU, a TEDx Speaker, and a Doctoral Candidate at the University of Southern California.
