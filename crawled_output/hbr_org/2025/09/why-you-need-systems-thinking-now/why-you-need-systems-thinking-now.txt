But many of those innovations have costs. For example, plastics are used to make many convenient and low-cost consumer products, but they create huge problems for both terrestrial and marine ecosystems and find their way into thousands of kinds of animals, including humans. Fracking technology has kept the price of oil low, but it has harmful effects on water resources and contributes to air pollution and other environmental problems.
