Taxpayer identification numbers (TIN)
Filing your Form 1040 federal income tax return.
Deadline
April 15, 2025 is the deadline to file and pay taxes.
File for free
File your taxes for free online with IRS Direct File or IRS Free File. Or file in person if you qualify.
How to file: step by step
Follow these steps to file your taxes.
Extension
Get an extension to October 15 to file your taxes. Pay by the April deadline to avoid penalties and interest. You may have more time to file if you were affected by a disaster situation.
Your account
Get your tax records, get an IP PIN and make a payment. Sign in or set up your account.
Tax records and transcripts
Get your tax records and transcripts.
Identity protection PIN (IP PIN)
Protect yourself from tax-related identity theft. Get an IP PIN.
Forms
Search all forms and instructions.
Taxpayer identification number (TIN)
To file federal taxes, you need a taxpayer identification number (TIN).
Life events
Employment, marriage and divorce, family size, retirement, disasters and other life events can affect your taxes.
