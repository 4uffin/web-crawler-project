Write for us!
Polygon is one of the world's most influential and highly sought-out sources of news, insight, and guidance in the wild world of gaming and digital entertainment adjacent to that space. We might be a brand that's on the tip of everyone's tongues when it comes to games journalism, but in reality, that potency is a mere reflection of what truly makes us awesome — the people whose thoughts, feelings, knowledge, and passion brings millions of readers to our digital doorstep on a daily basis.
Can I write for Polygon?
Short answer? Yes! Whether you're an industry veteran or you're just getting started, we're always on the prowl for bright, motivated talent looking to make their mark. From cutting-edge news and thoughtful op-eds to gameplay video specialists and more, we employ and contract with a variety of amazing content creators — if you've got the right stuff, you're welcome to join them!
What we're looking for...
Passion and experience with video games and the industry at large.
Ace-tier communication and collaboration.
Comfort and ease delivering your best on tight deadlines.
If you can tick those three boxes without thinking twice, then we want to hear from you.
