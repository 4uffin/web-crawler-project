Bryan Cranston also won the Emmy for outstanding guest actor in a comedy series for his performance as Griffin Mill in the series. "Severance” won six Emmys, including outstanding cinematography for a series (one hour) and outstanding music composition for a series (original dramatic score). Merritt Wever also won the Emmy for outstanding guest actress in a drama series for her performance as Gretchen George in the hit series.
