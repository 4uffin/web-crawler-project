Why Partner With HackerRank?
The HackerRank Partner Network allows you to deliver the best recruiting and talent management to the customers you serve. Our network spans multiple business types, including technology, services, and nonprofit organizations, to ensure total coverage of any need.
Deliver a great experience while showcasing a tech-forward organization aligned with customer values
Leverage technology and best practices to save time and make better hiring decisions
Find and attract diverse candidates and ensure a fair, unbiased hiring process
Create new relationships through our network of more than 3,000 customers

HackerRank Verified
Consulting organizations are helping clients achieve their innovation goals by contributing technical talent to expand and strengthen their bench of talent. Clients have an exponentially increasing requirement to have verified consultants work on their projects. To enable this, HackerRank has launched HackerRank Verified.
HackerRank Verified is a fast and efficient way for consulting organizations to build trust and demonstrate they have the expertise in leveraging HackerRank to accurately measure the skills of their consulting teams. The program also enables consultants to get to work on client projects sooner.
Become a partner
BENEFITS OF THE PROGRAM
Consulting organizations with Verified status are able to access:
Training on how to use HackerRank to generate skills data required for clients.
Profile on the HackerRank Partner Website
Inclusion in marketing promotion and material
Verified badge for marketing communication
