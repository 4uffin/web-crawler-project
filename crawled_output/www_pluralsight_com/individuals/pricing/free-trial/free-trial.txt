Frequently asked questions
How long does the free trial last?
When you sign up for a free trial, you’ll receive 10 days of Pluralsight access. If you don’t cancel within 10 days, the trial converts to a paid subscription that will auto-renew until it is canceled.
Will I be charged now for a free trial?
No, we won't charge you at this time. However, there is an immediate $1 USD hold on your account that will be reversed.
What’s included in the free trial?
The 10-day free trial gives you access to your learning package’s full course library, Skill IQ assessments, certification prep, guided learning paths, and a selection of our hands-on practice labs. To download courses offline, or access all of our practice labs/sandbox features, you’ll need to upgrade to a paid plan.
What happens to my course progress at the end of my free trial?
When your free trial ends, your Pluralsight subscription will automatically begin, allowing you to continue learning without interruption. All your coursework and progress from the trial will be saved in your account. If you cancel your free trial, you will lose access to your courses, but we'll save your progress and restore it if you sign up again in the future.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your free trial or subscription at any time. Your plan will auto-renew until you cancel.
Can I switch learning packages during my free trial?
To give you the best learning experience, you’ll choose a learning package at the start of your trial and have full access to its content. If you’d like to explore other topics, you can upgrade to the Complete plan at anytime or switch after your trial ends.
