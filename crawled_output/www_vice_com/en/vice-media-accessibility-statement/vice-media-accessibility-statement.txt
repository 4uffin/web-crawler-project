Vice Media Accessibility Statement
At VICE Digital Publishing, our goal is to build a company that mirrors the diversity of the world we shine a light on every day. There are no shortcuts. No silver bullet.
Creating a culture of equity and belonging is everyone’s responsibility, and that’s why we are working to create and maintain an accessible digital presence. Vice Digital Publishing is committed to digital accessibility, and to conforming to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level A and AA and complying with Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) effective communication requirements, and other applicable regulations.
We want to hear from you if you encounter any accessibility barriers on our digitalproperties. Please contact our Customer Support at accessibility@vice.com
