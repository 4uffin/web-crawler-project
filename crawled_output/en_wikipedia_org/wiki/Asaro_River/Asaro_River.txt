River in Papua New Guinea
Asaro River is a river in Eastern Highlands province, Papua New Guinea, located at 6°22′00″S 145°12′00″E﻿ / ﻿6.3666667°S 145.2°E﻿ / -6.3666667; 145.2. The Asaro is a tributary of the Tua, which in turn is a tributary of the Purari River.[1]
