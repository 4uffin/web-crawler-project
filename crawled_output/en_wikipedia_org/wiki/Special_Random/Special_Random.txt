Drenovo (Macedonian: Дреново) is a village in the municipality of Kavadarci, North Macedonia.
Demographics[edit]
On the 1927 ethnic map of Leonhard Schulze-Jena, the village is shown as a Muslims village.[1] According to the 2002 census, the village had a total of 648 inhabitants.[2] Ethnic groups in the village include:[2]
According to the census of 2021, 508 residents lived in the village, of which 438 were Macedonians, 1 Albanian, 1 Serb, and 68 people without data.
