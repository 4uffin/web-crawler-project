From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Algerian poet
Lakhdar Ben CherifBorn1899El-Oued, French Algeria[1]Died1967Bouzaréah, Algeria[1]
Lakhdar Ben Cherif (1899–1967) was a popular Algerian poet.[1] Many of his poems were later released as popular songs, including by the
Algerian singer Abdallah Menai.[1] Ben Cherif's mother was of Turkish origin.[1]
