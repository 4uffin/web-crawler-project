Hispodonta vicina is a species of beetle of the family Chrysomelidae. It is found in New Guinea.[1]
Description[edit]
Adults reach a length of about 9.4–10.6 mm. Adults are orange-red, with the posterior half of the disc greenish blue.
Biology[edit]
This species has been found feeding on Metroxylon species.[2]
