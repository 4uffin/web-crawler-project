Ip (Romanian: Ip; Hungarian: Ipp) is a commune in Sălaj County, Crișana, Romania. It is composed of five villages: Cosniciu de Jos (Alsókaznacs), Cosniciu de Sus (Felsőkaznacs), Ip, Zăuan (Szilágyzovány), and Zăuan-Băi (Zoványfürdő). The name Ip is the shortest name that an urban or rural locality bears in all of Romania, being the only name composed of only two letters.
The commune was the initial site of the events that led to the Ip massacre in 1940.
