Full details about the journal editors are available on our journal staff page. To contact the editorial office, please click here.
Manuscripts should be submitted through our online submission system. For more information, see our general guide for manuscript preparation and submission. Please do not send complete manuscripts by e-mail unless specifically requested.
Subscription
All subscription and customer service enquiries should be made directly to the relevant customer services departments. A comprehensive system of help pages and FAQs is available to assist with any enquiry. If you are unable to locate the required information you can send an email through the help pages to a dedicated customer service representative. Personal and institutional subscribers who require information about their subscriptions should also access the help pages.
Librarians
All information for librarians and other institutional subscribers can be found on our dedicated librarian website.
Institutional Partnerships
To partner with us for your needs in research strategy, communication, training, publishing and promotion, please see our separate Institutional Partnerships webpage. Nature Research Institutional Partnerships enable institutions to benefit from our editorial and communication expertise and reach a global audience of scientists, consumers and opinion-leaders leveraging the resources of Nature journals, Nature.com and Scientific American.
Advertising and strategic solutions from Nature
Nature Partnerships offers innovative solutions that connect your organisation directly with the global scientific community. Whether you aim to promote products, establish thought leadership, attract collaborators or guide research decisions, our tailored strategies help you achieve your goals.
We work with academic institutions, research-driven organisations and industry leaders to elevate brands, build world-class teams and showcase societal impact to our millions of readers.
Nature Careers
Nature Careers is the trusted recruitment platform from Nature, the destination for groundbreaking scientific discovery. Whether you're looking to hire top-tier researchers or elevate your employer brand, Nature Careers offers tailored solutions that reach millions of scientists worldwide—including active and passive jobseekers.
From job posts to employer branding and award, prize and event announcements, Nature Careers helps you position your organisation as a leader in the global research community.
