PLOS is a non-profit organization on a mission to drive open science forward with measurable, meaningful change in research publishing, policy, and practice.
Building on a strong legacy of pioneering innovation, PLOS continues to be a catalyst, reimagining models to meet open science principles, removing barriers and promoting inclusion in knowledge creation and sharing, and publishing research outputs that enable everyone to learn from, reuse and build upon scientific knowledge.
We believe in a better future where science is open to all, for all.

Editors and reviewers
A global community committed to making research robust, reliable, and accessible to all
Our journal editors and reviewers play a vital role in safeguarding the peer review process and ensuring ethical integrity. In return for your hard work and dedication, we provide recognition for the work you do, access to resources that will help you in your role, the opportunity to network with leading academics globally, and the chance to shape the future of research.
Your benefits and recognition
We recognize the benefits and contributions our editors and reviewers make to the research community.
By working with us as a journal editor or reviewer, you will:
Be part of a mission-driven, open science publisher that focuses on principles, not profits.Gain international recognition with your name published alongside each manuscript you work on.Be among the first in your field to see new research and influence what we publish.Earn credit through your ORCID and Web of Science researcher profiles.Have the opportunity to network with experts worldwide within your field of expertise, and beyond.Gain professional development experience as an editor or reviewer.
Join us and move open science forward
We place scientific rigor and ethics at the center of everything that we do
Our editors and reviewers are a global community that is committed to safeguarding research ethics and integrity and making sure that the research we publish is accurate and reliable.
We serve and support our communities
We understand the unique needs of the communities that we serve. This allows us to offer unique insights and support that will help you put your best foot forward. To support you in your role, we offer training and resources on a variety of topics.
We focus on principles, not profit
We exist to serve the research enterprise which means our priorities are based on the needs of scholars, librarians, funders, and institutional leaders, not shareholders. We will not waste public money and will do everything we can to ensure our services are valuable and cost effective.
Join our community of editors and reviewers advancing ethical researchWe are always looking for researchers who want to contribute to a more open, ethical, and rigorous scientific process. Whether you are interested in serving as an editor or a reviewer, we want to hear from you.
