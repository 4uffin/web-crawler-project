PLOS is a non-profit organization on a mission to drive open science forward with measurable, meaningful change in research publishing, policy, and practice.
Building on a strong legacy of pioneering innovation, PLOS continues to be a catalyst, reimagining models to meet open science principles, removing barriers and promoting inclusion in knowledge creation and sharing, and publishing research outputs that enable everyone to learn from, reuse and build upon scientific knowledge.
We believe in a better future where science is open to all, for all.

Discover smart cities research from PLOS
Cities are hubs of human interaction and the innovations that stem from them. While urbanization has brought numerous benefits to society, it has also contributed to some of our most urgent challenges, including sustainability, climate change, poverty, and inequality.The below PLOS articles explore the multi-disciplinary research around smart cities and their communities on a global scale. They cover a broad range of essential topics, including Internet of Things (IoT) infrastructure, machine learning, energy efficiency, and urban logistics, providing insights into how smart technologies can transform urban spaces into dynamic, resilient, and future-ready communities.
