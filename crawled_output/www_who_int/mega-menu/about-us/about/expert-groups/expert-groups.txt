Networks, committees, advisory groups, working groups and task forces
One of WHO’s key roles is to convene public health experts to consider and make recommendations on issues relating to the improvement of people’s health. Such experts are brought together through networks, committees, advisory groups, working groups and task forces. Below is a non-exhaustive list of these groups.
