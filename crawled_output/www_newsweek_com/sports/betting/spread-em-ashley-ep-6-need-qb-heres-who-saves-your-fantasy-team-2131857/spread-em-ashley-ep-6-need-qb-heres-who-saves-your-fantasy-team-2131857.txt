We're heading into Week 3 of the NFL, and the quarterback injuries just keep piling up. After San Francisco's Brock Purdy went down in Week 1, Cincinnati's Joe Burrow, Minnesota's J.J. McCarthy, Washington's Jayden Daniels and Jets QB Justin Fields all suffered injuries in Week 2.This episode of Spread 'Em breaks down what all these injuries mean for teams, spreads, and maybe more importantly, your fantasy lineups.
