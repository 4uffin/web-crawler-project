NewsweekCareersAt Newsweek, innovation is at the heart of everything we do. Our diverse global teams bring together unique perspectives, fostering an environment where every team member is encouraged to contribute meaningfully and drive impact. Our commitment to innovation means we are always exploring new ways to tell stories and connect with our audience. Join us and be part of a dynamic team that is dedicated to making a difference through cutting-edge journalism and collaborative efforts.Letter from our Global Editor in ChiefNewsweek has continually evolved and reinvented itself over more than 90 years, but our core principles remain steadfast. Our stories illuminate the world around us, offering fresh perspectives on the most significant events of our time. We seek common ground and are committed to delivering factual, fair, and honest journalism, clearly distinguishing opinion from reporting and openly acknowledging our mistakes and shortcomings. Our mission is more vital than ever in these times of unprecedented change.However, the political culture and media landscape have shifted. There's less common ground and more partisanship, polarization, and suspicion.Finding common ground is not about diluting our beliefs or avoiding conflict. It's about disagreeing without disrespect, debating without demeaning-- and engaging without enmity. It's about fostering an environment where constructive dialogue can flourish.At Newsweek, we strive for engagement, not agreement. We cover stories from multiple viewpoints, challenging unexamined assumptions, and conventional wisdom thoughtfully, factually, and respectfully. We believe this commitment makes Newsweek indispensable to our readers today.I appreciate your interest, and we look forward to hearing from you!
Our Core ValuesIntegrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency in our journalism.Accountability: We take ownership of our work and strive to exceed expectations.Curiosity: We ask tough questions and explore all angles to uncover the truth.Audience-First Mindset: We listen to our readers and prioritize their needs in all we do.Continuous Learning: We challenge ourselves to grow, adapt, and improve every day.Diversity & InclusionAs an equal opportunity employer, diversity and inclusion are at the forefront of our culture.We are committed to creating a workplace where every individual feels valued, respected, and empowered to contribute their unique perspectives. Our commitment to inclusion begins with our recruitment process. By embracing diverse backgrounds, experiences, and ideas, we strengthen our impact and connection to the communities we serve. As a global company, we reach candidates across the United States, Europe, Latin America and Asia to source a diverse pool for our digital newsroom, as well as for our Technology, Product, Commercial teams, and operations (Legal, Finance and People) teams.In the summer of 2023, Newsweek proudly received its NSMDC certification (National Minority Supplier Development Council), highlighting our status as a minority-owned and operated company.At Newsweek, we don't just offer jobs; we offer exciting opportunities to grow, innovate, and excel within an inclusive, equitable, and respectful workplace.Jobs at NewsweekAre you ready to make a difference?
