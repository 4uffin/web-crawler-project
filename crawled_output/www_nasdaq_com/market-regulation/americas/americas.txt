At Nasdaq, our regulatory functions operate independently of Nasdaq’s business units to safeguard the integrity of our markets.
Nasdaq Operates a Multifaceted Regulatory Program
Real-time surveillance of 3 equities and 6 options markets
Post-trade surveillance of those equities and options markets
Surveillance of Nasdaq PHLX’s trading floor
Issuer listing and on-going compliance for companies listed on Nasdaq
Membership application reviews and compliance of firms that trade on our equities and options markets
Testing of exchange trading systems to confirm alignment with exchange rules
Investigation and enforcement of potential market violations
