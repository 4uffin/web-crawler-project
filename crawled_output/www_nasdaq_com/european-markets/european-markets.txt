FAQs
What is Nasdaq Europe?
Nasdaq Europe refers to Nasdaq’s operations and stock exchanges located in Europe, including Nasdaq Stockholm, Nasdaq Copenhagen, Nasdaq Helsinki, Nasdaq Iceland, Nasdaq Tallinn, Nasdaq Riga, and Nasdaq Vilnius. These exchanges provide financial services and enable investors to buy and trade securities
What types of solutions does Nasdaq offer for European markets?
Nasdaq offers a comprehensive suite of modular solutions for listing services, risk management, clearing and settlement systems, surveillance, and advanced trading platforms.
Nasdaq trading platforms support global equities, options, futures, bonds, and structured products.
Explore our cutting-edge technology solutions tailored for European markets.
How do I list my company on Nasdaq's European markets?
Discover the listing process and benefits of having your company listed on Nasdaq's European markets in the Listings section.
Start your listing journey here: Nasdaq Nordic First North Growth Market
Can international companies list on Nasdaq's European markets?
Yes, Nasdaq´s European markets welcome international companies to list on their exchanges.
The process and requirements for international companies to list may vary, so it is recommended to refer to the guidelines and support resources for detailed information on listing eligibility and procedures on Nasdaq's European exchanges:
Nasdaq Nordic Main Market Regulations
Nasdaq Nordic First North Growth Market Regulations
Can I trade international securities on Nasdaq's European markets?
Yes, Nasdaq´s European markets offer opportunities to trade international securities.
However, specific availability depends on the exchange and offerings.
Learn more about trading global securities in accordance with our Market Rules & Regulations
Can I find stock market prices on Nasdaq.com for European companies?
Yes, Nasdaq.com provides stock market proces for many European companies listed on its European echanges in the Nordic and Baltic regions.Explore the latest prices here: European Market Activity
What are the trading hours for Nasdaq's European markets?
Nasdaq operates during regular trading hours, but specific timings can vary.
Explore the Trading hours page to understand the schedules, market segments, holidays, and any special trading sessions that may affect regular hours.
The detailed trading hours are set out in the respective applicable Market Rules & Regulations
How can investors access market data for European markets on Nasdaq?
Investors seeking market data for European markets on Nasdaq can access information through our dedicated Market Data section on this page.
How can I contact Nasdaq's European markets support team?
Navigate to the “Contact Us” section to get in touch with Nasdaq's support team for inquiries, assistance, and information related to Nasdaq´s European markets.
