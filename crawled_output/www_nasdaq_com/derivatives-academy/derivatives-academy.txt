Frequently Asked Questions
Download FAQ->
What is Nasdaq Derivatives Academy?
Nasdaq Derivatives Academy is an intuitive on-demand e-learning program, offering a variety of courses developed to fit your needs. The course offering consists of three separate tracks: Private Investors, Professional Investors, and Risk, Post Trade, and Compliance Personnel.
Why is Nasdaq launching this initiative?
To satisfy the explicit need for neutral, high quality and cost-efficient education on equity derivatives. Following thorough market analysis and interviews with countless members across the Nordic equity derivatives market, it’s been clear that there is a pressing need to learn more about what equity derivatives are and the risks and opportunities associated with it.
Who is Nasdaq Derivatives Academy suitable for?
Whether you're just starting out or are an experienced derivatives trader, our courses help investors succeed at all stages of their investment expertise. Discover more about our offering and find the right course for you above.
How does Nasdaq Derivatives Academy work?
When is the course taking place?
The courses are fully digital and offered on-demand to best fit your schedule.
How do I access the courses?
All you have to do is go to our website and register an account.
Will there be any course diploma available?
Yes, upon completion of the course you’ll be awarded with a Nasdaq Derivatives sponsored diploma as well as a LinkedIn badge.
How long does it take to go through a course and pass the exam?
Estimated times for completion can be found under the course descriptions on our website.
I don’t have any experience in equity derivatives, can I still take the course?
Yes, the course for Private Investors: Options Trading for Private Investors covers all the basics you need to know about derivatives and doesn’t require previous knowledge. After completing the course and passing the exam, you will have knowledge that will help you make your first derivatives trade.
I already have the basic knowledge in equity derivatives, is there a more advanced course I can take?
The course for Professional Investors: Derivatives for Professionals, offers extensive information and in-depth knowledge whether in trading, investment or hedging purposes. Among other outcomes, you’ll learn how to generate alpha, create more efficient cash management, create proper risk controls, and further equip yourself for complex investment decisions.
Do I need to have an account open with a broker before I start the course?
An open brokerage account is not a requirement to take the course, yet it will be advantageous as you will learn to incorporate your theoretical and case-study knowledge in a practical setting.
I’m interested in leveraged products, is this course relevant for me?
Absolutely. ETP’s, options, and futures share similar characteristics, such as use of leverage, but with differences in cost, risk structure, and liquidity. We’ll outline benefits of utilizing options and futures in your portfolio throughout the course also from a leverage perspective.
Do I need to download any specific app in order to take the course?
No, all you need is an internet connection and a web browser.
Will there be any support for attendees of the course?
External resources, such as OptionsPlay, are also provided to Nordic retail investors free of charge.
What happens after the course, will there be any follow-ups?
After completing the course, you’ll be offered the opportunity to become a member of Nasdaq Derivatives Academy’s community. The community consist of individuals and professionals with a keen interest in derivatives trading. As a member of the Nasdaq Derivatives Academy community, you'll be offered exclusive invitations to various seminars, webinars, physical gatherings and more.
