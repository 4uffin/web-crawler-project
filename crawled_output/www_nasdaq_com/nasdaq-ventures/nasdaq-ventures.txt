Nasdaq Ventures
Nasdaq Ventures is a global venture investing program focused on cultivating talent and technology advancement within financial services–spurring innovation that aligns with and accelerates our vision to reimagine the markets of tomorrow.
We invest in startups across seven key themes:
Market Infrastructure
Support the modernization of market infrastructure across asset classes & geographies
Data, Analytics, & Workflow Technologies
Empower clients with useful insights and efficient workflows
Anti-Financial Crime
Enhance the integrity of the global financial system
Blockchain & Digital Assets
Foster institutional adoption of blockchain and digital assets
Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG)
Support corporates and investors in their ESG and decarbonization goals
New Marketplaces
Increase efficiency of non-financial asset classes by improving price discovery and lowering transaction costs
Enabling Technologies
Emerging technologies such as AI and quantum computing with fintech use cases
Investment Criteria
Strategic Nexus with Nasdaq
Strategically aligned with one or more of Nasdaq's business units either directly (i.e. a revenue or distribution synergy, joint product development) or indirectly (thematically relevant)
Large Market Opportunity
Operates in a large and growing market with well-defined customer segments and strong secular tailwinds
Experienced Team
Strong management team with complementary skills, aligned incentives, relevant industry experience, and diverse backgrounds; ideally founders with operating experience
Commercial Traction
Well-defined go-to-market strategy and market validation from early clients
Investment Size
Nasdaq Ventures will typically lead or co-lead seed, Series A, or Series B financings
