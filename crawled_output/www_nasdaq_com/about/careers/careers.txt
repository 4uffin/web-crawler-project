Get to Know Nasdaq
We Power the Markets that Power the World
Nasdaq is a global technology company that delivers world-leading platforms that improve the integrity, transparency and liquidity of the global economy.Through our daily work, we power stronger economies, create more equitable opportunities and contribute to a more sustainable world to help our communities, clients, employees and people of all backgrounds reach their full potential.Since 1971, when Nasdaq created the world’s first electronic exchange, our innovations have revolutionized the global capital markets. Join us and help advance economic progress for all.

Nasdaq's Significant Operations
Nasdaq operates in 38 countries across the world. We have significant operations (which we define as more than 200 employees in a location) in the U.S., Canada, Sweden, Lithuania, India, Philippines, Australia and the United Kingdom. These eight locations comprise over 85% of our total employee population.

Benefits & Rewards
We offer a variety of programs, benefits, and resources to support our employees' priorities and goals at work and in their personal lives. With a mix of globally consistent and locally tailored programs, we strive to meet everyone’s needs, wherever they live.
Health
Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision care, paid time off, flexible work arrangements
Wealth
Competitive base salary, retirement plans (6% 401(k) match for US employees), equity grants, tuition reimbursement, student loan assistance
Community
Paid volunteer days, matched donations, 11 employee networks, employee recognition program
Career
Mentoring program, leadership development courses, Udemy membership
Family
Parental leave, fertility assistance, child/elder care support
Leveraging the
70/20/10 Learning Principle
At Nasdaq, 70% of development happens on the job, 20% through coaching & mentoring and 10% by formal training.
