Spotlighted Solutions
Nasdaq Global Index Data Service
->
Nasdaq Global Index Data Service (GIDS) is Nasdaq’s real-time data feed that consolidates all Nasdaq indexes, as well as exchange-traded products (ETPs) valuation data and third-party index data. The widespread distribution to over 600 global firms promotes capital performance transparency and education for investors, asset managers and the financial community worldwide.
Nasdaq Fund Network
->
The Nasdaq Fund Network (NFN) facilitates the collection and dissemination of performance, NAV, valuation, and strategy-level reference data for about 45,000 investable products to 100 million plus investors. Product issuers and asset managers benefit from an automated service that helps bring more awareness and gathers assets for new and existing products. Institutional and retail investors, as well as the general investing public, benefit from daily access to transparent fund-level data for a wide range of products to help inform investment decisions.
Bruce ATS™
->
Bruce Alternative Trading System (ATS) offers after-hours access to U.S. markets, specifically for Reg National Market System (NMS) securities. Built for enterprise-scale needs and leveraging industry-leading Nasdaq technology, Bruce delivers a fully digital overnight trading solution designed for optimal reliability and performance.
Bruce Last Sale, Bruce Best Bid and Offer, and Bruce Depth-of-Book are all available via Nasdaq Data Link today!
Nasdaq Mutual Fund Reports
->
Nasdaq Fund Network's (NFN) Mutual Fund Reports Data provides comprehensive coverage of the closed-end fund and open-end mutual fund universes. The data is collected directly from fund sponsors and regulatory filings and can be used for research and fintech solutions. The data set is also enriched with appropriate identifiers and reference data. The dataset is updated daily and is designed for consistency with NFN pricing data.
Sharadar
->
Sharadar specializes in extraction, standardization and organization of US financial data from company filings including: equity and fund pricing, company fundamentals, insider and institutional holdings and more with data going back over 20 years.
