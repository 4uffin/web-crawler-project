Nasdaq Center for Board Excellence
A community dedicated to advancing corporate leadership
Areas of Expertise
Corporate Governance,
Board and Leadership,
Economy,
ESG,
Cybersecurity,
Indexes Research and Insights
The Nasdaq Center for Board Excellence is a community and collaboration environment, in which board engagement is deepened and experiences are shared. By introducing leaders across organizations, industries, and regions, we offer the opportunity to gain informed insights and promote board excellence. Leaders who join the community have access to participate in, contribute to, and learn through exclusive interactive events, educational resources, and engaging experiences.
To learn more and sign up to join the Nasdaq Center for Board Excellence community, visit nasdaq.com/solutions/governance/center-for-board-excellence.
