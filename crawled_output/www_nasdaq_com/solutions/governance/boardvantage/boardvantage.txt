What is a board portal?
A board portal is software for streamlining corporate governance processes. This must-have software lets boards operate entirely paperlessly, enhancing meeting effectiveness, collaboration, and decision-making in corporate governance. Our board portal, Nasdaq Boardvantage®, is designed for boards, committees, and executive leadership teams and features robust security features and a user-friendly experience.
What are the benefits of a board portal?
A board portal, like all board management software, is built to help you work more efficiently, productively, and strategically. A board portal helps improve management of meetings and materials, collaboration, decision-making, and corporate governance processes—all with added security.
