Powering Trading and Investment Decisions Globally
Nasdaq is the preeminent global market data provider sourcing data from equity markets across North America and Europe, including our flagship Nasdaq stock market, the largest liquidity pool by market share in the US. Building off this foundation, our North America equity data solutions represent differentiated depth and accuracy.
Why Nasdaq North American Equity Data Solutions?
Nasdaq accounts for about 60% of the U.S. equity market trading volume. With this significant market share and advanced technology, Nasdaq's equity data products are designed to help consumers make more informed investment decisions and drive greater transparency into the markets.
Real-Time and Historical Data
Access market data on a real-time basis or back test with historical data.
Comprehensive Suite of Solutions
Offering Level 1, Level 2, exchange reports, and other market data datasets.
Superior Accuracy
Access accurate and timely data with Nasdaq. Nasdaq's last trade matches the SIP 81% of the time and deviates on average by only $0.004 when not aligned, underscoring its relative superior accuracy.
Seamless Delivery via API
Nasdaq Data Link's cloud-based APIs provide real-time data directly with effortless integration.
Get the Most Complete Picture
Nasdaq's U.S. equity data solutions, including Nasdaq Last Sale Plus and Nasdaq Basic, integrate off-exchange trading data from Nasdaq's TRFs, accounting for as much as 40% of total U.S. trade volume. Get the most complete picture with Nasdaq U.S. equity data, covering over 60% of the market.
