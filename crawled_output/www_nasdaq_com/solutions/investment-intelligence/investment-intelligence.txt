Nasdaq Investment Intelligence
Question today. To rewrite tomorrow.
What is a decision without transparency, data, and insight?
about Nasdaq Investment Intelligence
Nasdaq’s Investment Intelligence unit is focused on enabling economic growth through access to capital and transparency. We are specifically anchored in the ability to provide clients with the insights they need to make the most informed investment decisions in an increasingly complex and fast-moving capital markets environment.
From asset managers investing on behalf of the largest global institutions, to financial advisors investing individual investors’ hard earned retirement funds, we empower intelligent investment decisions with the best possible data and insights. Our solutions help our clients strengthen economies for the benefit of individuals around the globe.
Our Solutions
Our unique blend of world-class data, SaaS workflow solutions and innovative indexes propel informed decisions, while our cutting-edge liquidity solutions are making private markets more accessible and efficient.
Nasdaq Data Link
Our proprietary exchange data, asset manager data, and alternative data form the foundation of our robust offering. These gold-standard data solutions are designed for firms at every stage of their lifecycles. We power a range of industries including fintechs, data & media vendors, asset managers, banks, and retail brokers. Our data is delivered directly to clients via cutting edge technology and APIs, or through a global ecosystem of partners. This allows our clients to leverage new insights within their existing workflows and without disruption.

Nasdaq eVestment and Nasdaq Solovis
Data, transparency, and empowering informed investment decisions is at the heart of cutting-edge Nasdaq eVestment and Nasdaq Solovis platforms. These platforms transform data into the analysis and actionable insights that asset managers, institutional investors, and investment consultants need to make intelligent decisions. With Nasdaq eVestment data and analytics at their fingertips, users can discern trends, anticipate shifts, manage due diligence, formulate responses, and conduct manager selection processes to foster growth and keep them a step ahead in the everchanging investment landscape. Nasdaq Solovis then allows allocators to understand performance, holdings, exposures, and risk in real time across all asset classes.
