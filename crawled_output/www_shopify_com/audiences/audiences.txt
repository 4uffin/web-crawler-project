This custom advertising solution is powered by millions of commerce insights only available on Shopify.Install AudiencesFrequently asked questionsWho can use Shopify Audiences?Shopify Plus merchants using Shopify Payments and operating in the United States or Canada can activate Audiences at any time. Log in to your Shopify Plus account to get started. If you’re not on Shopify Plus, you can upgrade your plan.How much does Shopify Audiences cost?Shopify Audiences is currently free to install and use for all eligible Shopify Plus stores. When you activate a campaign, you pay the partner platform directly for the advertising costs.How do I set up Shopify Audiences?Visit the Shopify app store and install the Shopify Audiences app. Once installed, follow the simple on-screen steps. For more guidance visit the Shopify Audiences help documentation.How does Shopify Audiences create audience lists?Shopify Audiences uses the collective power of participating merchants to help drive better ad performance. In order to use Shopify Audiences, merchants must opt-in to contribute data to the Shopify Network Intelligence. The Shopify Audiences algorithms analyze the data in this network to develop
millions of buyer behavior
insights. The algorithms match buyer intent with the unique attributes of your store to create a list of potential customers. Audience lists are based on how likely buyers are to purchase from your store. No other merchant can see another merchant’s customer data or directly access information on any buyer. For more information take a look at our help documentation on this topic.How do I use audience lists in my ad campaigns?After you connect to an ad platform using the Shopify Audiences app, audiences are automatically created and exported to your ad account. Several objective-based types of audiences are created and exported for you to use in your ad campaigns. To use an audience for an ad campaign, select it as the target when you create a campaign on your ad platform.How can I respect customers’ privacy while using Shopify Audiences? Shopify Audiences was built to enable you to comply with applicable privacy laws of the region. To share data that you will use for Shopify Audiences, you need to obtain any applicable permissions and consents, as required by applicable laws and industry guidelines.
