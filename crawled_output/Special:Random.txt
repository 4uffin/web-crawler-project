Village in Vladimir Oblast, RussiaStarinki
СтаринкиVillageStarinkiShow map of Vladimir OblastStarinkiShow map of RussiaCoordinates: 56°20′N 38°25′E﻿ / ﻿56.333°N 38.417°E﻿ / 56.333; 38.417[1]CountryRussiaRegionVladimir OblastDistrictAlexandrovsky DistrictTime zoneUTC+3:00
Starinki (Russian: Старинки) is a rural locality (a village) in Karinskoye Rural Settlement, Alexandrovsky District, Vladimir Oblast, Russia. The population was 1 as of 2010.[2] There is 1 street.
Geography[edit]
The village is located 21 km south-west from Bolshoye Karinskoye, 25 km south-west from Alexandrov.
