From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Argentine composer
Gaby Kerpel is a composer born and raised in Argentina.[1]
Kerpel belongs to a Latin electronic music collective known as Zizek.[2]
Under the name King Coya he performs reinterpreted Colombian cumbia music.[2]
As a composer, his songs include De La Guarda, Fuerza Bruta,
and Carnabailito.
