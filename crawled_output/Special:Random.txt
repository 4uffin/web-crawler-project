Railway station in Huaihua, Hunan
Xinhuang新晃General informationLocationXinhuang Dong Autonomous County, Huaihua, HunanChinaCoordinates27°21′06″N 109°11′31″E﻿ / ﻿27.351750°N 109.191984°E﻿ / 27.351750; 109.191984Line(s)Shanghai–Kunming railwayHistoryOpened1972Location
Xinhuang railway station (Chinese: 新晃站) is a railway station in Xinhuang Dong Autonomous County, Huaihua, Hunan, China. It is an intermediate stop on the Hunan–Guizhou section of the Shanghai–Kunming railway. It opened in 1972 and is under China Railway Guangzhou Group.[1][2]
