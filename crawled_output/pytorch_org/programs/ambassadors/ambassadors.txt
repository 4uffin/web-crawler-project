PyTorch Ambassadors
The PyTorch Ambassador Program highlights and supports passionate community leaders who educate, advocate for, and build with PyTorch in meaningful ways.
Ambassadors help grow PyTorch usage around the world by organizing events, creating educational content, mentoring new users, and contributing to the open-source ecosystem.
The first official cohort of PyTorch Ambassadors will launch in September 2025.
What Does a PyTorch Ambassador Do?
PyTorch Ambassadors are trusted, independent voices who:
Organize local and virtual PyTorch community events
Create technical content, tutorials, or videos
Mentor new users and promote inclusive community growth
Represent PyTorch at conferences, meetups, and in research or academic settings
Why Become a PyTorch Ambassador?
The program offers recognition, resources, and support for leaders who want to make a greater impact through:
Community engagement and mentorship
Technical content and knowledge sharing
Event hosting and public speaking
Recognition & Support
PyTorch Ambassadors receive:
Official recognition from the PyTorch Foundation
Promotion via the PyTorch website and social channels
Swag and reimbursement for approved community activity expenses
Access to exclusive training and briefings
Opportunities to speak at events and conferences
Private collaboration channels with other ambassadors and Foundation staff
