God of War fans can save big on the recently released 20th Anniversary Retrospective for a limited time. Published by Dark Horse in July, the two-volume hardcover box set is available for 33% off, dropping the price of the $100 collection to only $66.33. It's unclear when Amazon's limited-time promotion ends, so snag the commemorative collection for its best price yet while you can. The Deluxe Edition has also received a roughly $30 discount.God of War: 20th Anniversary Retrospective$66.33 ($100) | Limited-time DealGod of War: 20th Anniversary RetrospectiveThe God of War: 20th Anniversary Retrospective Set is a two-volume hardcover book collection featuring 320 pages of artwork, behind-the-scenes materials, and exclusive interviews with key members of each game's development team. Both hardcovers and the ornate slipcase are wrapped in leatherette.Volume 1: Greek Saga covers the original God of War Trilogy and its numerous spin-offs, while Volume 2: Norse Saga details the development of God of War 2016 and God of War: Ragnarok. The 20th Anniversary Retrospective was written by Rick Barba, the author behind God of War: Lore and Legends, a recreation of Atreus' journal published by Dark Horse in 2020. Get deal at Amazon God of War: 20th Anniversary Retrospective - Deluxe Edition$169.28 ($200) | Available NowGod of War: 20th Anniversary Retrospective Deluxe EditionThe Deluxe Edition has a few flourishes and additional materials that will likely appeal to collectors and massive God of War fans. Both books have gilded page edges, ribbed spines, slightly different cover art, built-in ribbon bookmarks, and a display box with unique gold-foil illustrations on the front and back.The Deluxe Edition also comes with a bifold portfolio containing a pair of lithographs. One of the prints features artwork inspired by the PlayStation 2 games, and the other is based on the contemporary reboot. Get deal at Amazon

Officially licensed Kratos and Atreus action figures
God of War fans should also check out the official Kratos and Atreus action figures in the PlayStation Shapes Collection. Released last year, the 6-inch figures have numerous points of articulation and come with swappable accessories.
