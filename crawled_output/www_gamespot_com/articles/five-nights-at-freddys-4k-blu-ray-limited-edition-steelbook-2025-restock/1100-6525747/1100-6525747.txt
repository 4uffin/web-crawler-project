The popular Five Nights at Freddy's 4K Steelbook Edition is coming back to major retailers on November 11. That's good news for anyone who didn't snag a copy of the original before resellers started selling it for $60 or more. The reprint of Five Nights at Freddy's Steelbook Edition retails for $35, but it's available to preorder for a $5 discount at Walmart. This edition comes with 4K Blu-ray, 1080p Blu-ray, and digital versions of the hit 2023 film.The reprint arrives just in time for a rewatch ahead of Five Nights at Freddy's 2's December 5 theatrical release. But the Steelbook Edition isn't the only upcoming collectible edition of the first movie. On October 7, Five Nights at Freddy's Ultimate Collector's Edition launches exclusively at Amazon with a steelbook case of its own and several other collectibles. Both editions include the same on-disc content as Five Nights at Freddy's Night Shift Edition, which is available now for $27 (was $34).Check out both upcoming collectible editions below.
Five Nights at Freddy's Limited Edition Steelbook (4K Blu-ray)
$30 | Releases November 11
This year's version of the Steelbook Edition matches the original with cartoon-style character art of Freddy Fazbear, Chica, Foxy, and Bonnie. Don't worry, Chica didn't lose Cupcake; the little animatronic pastry is on the back of the case. The interior artwork is a collection of drawings from the walls of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza in the movie.The 4K Blu-ray disc supports HDR10 and two surround sound formats: Dolby Atmos and Dolby TrueHD 7.1. The voucher for the digital edition is Movies Anywhere, so you can download and watch it on any device.Special features include a trio of featurettes: Five Nights at Freddy's: From Game to Big ScreenKiller AnimatronicsFive Nights in Three Dimensions
Preorder at Amazon
Preorder at Walmart
Five Nights at Freddy's: Ultimate Collector's Edition (4K Blu-ray)
$75 | Releases October 7 | Amazon Exclusive
Part of Amazon's Ultimate Collector Series, this edition comes with an IconArt Metal Poster (shown above), an exclusive steelbook case with character art pulled from the film, and a Freddy Fazbear's Pizza Box. You'll also get a numbered certificate of authenticity, and unlike the Steelbook Edition above, this version won't be reprinted.The Ultimate Collector's Edition costs more than double the price of the Steelbook Edition reprint.Includes:Exclusive SteelbookIconArt Metal Poster + Magnet Wall MountFreddy Fazbear's Pizza BoxNumbered certificate of authenticity
