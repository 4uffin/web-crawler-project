DC's annual celebration of Batman kicked off last week and runs through September 23. Amazon is observing Batman Day 2025 by offering superb deals on Batman movie and TV series box sets. Many of the limited-time deals are even better than the discounts Amazon offered during Prime Day 2025. For instance, The Dark Knight Trilogy's 4K Blu-ray box set is only $29, the Batman 1989-1997 4-Film 4K Collection is $33, and Batman: The Complete Animated Series is just $29.49. We separated Amazon's Batman movie and TV deals into sections to make it easy to browse the lists below. If you want to see all of the deals in one place, we created an Amazon hub collecting all of the best Batman Blu-ray deals.

Batman Animated TV Series & Movie Box Set Deals On the animated side of the franchise, there are a bunch of standout box set deals. Batman: The Complete Animated Series is up for grabs for just $29.49 (was $80). This collection comes with all 85 episodes of the best animated series featuring the Caped Crusader. You'll also get every episode in The New Batman Adventures, bringing the total number of episodes to 109. As an added bonus, this set comes with two movies: Batman: Mask of the Phantasm and Batman and Mr. Freeze.
