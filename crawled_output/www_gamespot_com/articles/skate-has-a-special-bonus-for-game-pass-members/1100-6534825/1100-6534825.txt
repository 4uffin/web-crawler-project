Xbox Game Pass subscribers get "perks" for certain games, and the newly launched Skate is among them.Game Pass subscribers can grab the Supercharge Pack, which includes one deck art, one set of trucks, one set of wheels, and five stickers. Players must claim this perk by November 14 and redeem it by November 21.This perk was not mentioned in Microsoft's recent Game Pass blog post, but the Skate perk is viewable n the Xbox app on mobile. Some players are reporting issues accessing the Supercharge Pack, however.Get rewarded.Skate is a free-to-play, always-online game that's available now for PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X|S. The game is heading to iOS and Android later on, but there is no word about Switch 2. Skate is available now as an early-access game, and it won't get its "full" release for about a year. Full Circle plans to collect feedback from players to help improve the experience for whenever the game is ready for its 1.0 debut.
