Global Rights
Springer's record of innovation and our long history as a publisher of high quality books and journals make Springer content highly valued for translation, republication and reprint by publishing houses, commercial organisations, academic institutions, not-for-profits and individuals all over the world. We invite you to take a closer look and contact our rights team to discuss how we can help you find titles for your publishing program.
