Exact requirements vary by state.What's the difference between a counselor and a psychologist?A psychologist typically has a doctoral degree while a counselor usually has a master's degree. Additionally, counselors help clients work through life challenges using a holistic, conversational approach. Psychologists focus on a client's thoughts, behaviors, and emotions as they relate to long-term patterns and potential mental health conditions.How is counseling different from therapy?Counseling is a humanistic form of talk therapy that helps clients resolve the challenges they currently face in their everyday lives. Therapy tends to explore how a client's past informs their present through the lens of behavioral psychology.How much does an LPC make?According to the BLS, the lowest-earning 10% of substance use and mental health counselors made less than $36,700, and the highest-earning 10% made more than $89,920 in 2023.
