Frequently asked questions
Who can collaborate with Codecademy?
We work with companies, nonprofits, and organizations that share our mission to empower tech learners worldwide. Whether you’re a leading brand looking to engage with a global community of upskillers, or a nonprofit focused on expanding access to digital education, we’d love to explore opportunities together. This is separate from our affiliate program, which publishers and content creators looking to earn commission can join.
How much does it cost?
It depends! Many of our partnerships have been completely free through in-kind collaborations, but we’re open to discussing other kinds of partnerships with brands looking to amplify their reach.
What kind of collaborations are possible?
There are a variety of ways we can co-create curriculum like courses, videos, projects, and tutorials for our platform. We also can offer discounts or sponsorships for Pro, Plus, or Teams memberships, host co-branded giveaways or events, and more. If you’re not sure what you’re looking for, we’d be happy to answer questions or guide you through options.
