We approach sustainability from many angles.
From our early commitment to reducing our environmental impact to our ongoing efforts to provide high-quality sustainability data, analytics and benchmarks, Bloomberg has long been focused on shaping a sustainable future. As the global economy evolves, we’re continuing to reduce our greenhouse gas emissions, support our clients, develop our employees and give back to our communities.

We’re identifying sustainability opportunities across our operations
We are identifying sustainability opportunities across our operations. We’ve set science-based targets and are working towards reducing our greenhouse gas emissions footprint. We’ve pledged to match 100% of our annual global electricity consumption with renewable energy by 2025.
Our European headquarters was rated one of the world’s most sustainable office buildings upon its completion in 2017.
