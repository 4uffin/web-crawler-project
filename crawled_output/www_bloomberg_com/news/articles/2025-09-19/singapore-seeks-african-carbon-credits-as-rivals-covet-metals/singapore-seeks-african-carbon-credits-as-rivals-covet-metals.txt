Singapore’s main target is different: the continent’s carbon offsets. The Southeast Asian city-state aims to increasingly tap African countries for carbon credits as part of a drive to boost trade with the continent and meet its own net zero ambitions. At the same time it’s seeking to establish itself as a carbon-trading hub for Asian companies seeking to buy the offsets to meet their climate targets.
