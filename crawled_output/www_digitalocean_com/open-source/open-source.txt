If you haven't adopted one of these yet, you may not be quite ready for sponsorship. Consider researching your options. Here are two great resources for that:Understanding Open Source Software LicensesGuidance on Choosing a LicenseWhat if my project doesn't have a Code of Conduct?We will consider projects without a Code of Conduct however we highly encourage you to create one. Our friends at Indeed created this great resource on how to write your own: https://www.indeed.com/hire/c/info/write-a-code-of-conduct.How do I apply?Fill out and submit the application form.When will I be notified of the decision on my application?We review all applications and notify applicants of our decisions within 7-10 business days.Will I be notified when it's time to renew?Yes. You will begin to receive email reminder notifications beginning 65 days prior to the expiry of your grant. You may submit a renewal request for credit sponsorship via the application form.Am I guaranteed to be renewed if I've received support in the past?No. All applications are subject to our review and approval process. You will be asked to fill out the Credits for Open Source Application each time you renew. If the project renewal is declined you will be informed before your grant runs out. It is helpful for us to understand why you need the renewal, and if you have new or different usage needs.
