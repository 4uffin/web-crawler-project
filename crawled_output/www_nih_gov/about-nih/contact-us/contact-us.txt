The information and email services on this website cannot provide personalized medical advice about conditions or treatments. The resources on this site should not be used as a substitute for professional medical care. Contact your medical care providers for answers to personal health questions. We also do not rate or provide referrals to physicians or health care facilities. Such activities are usually handled by state or local governments.
