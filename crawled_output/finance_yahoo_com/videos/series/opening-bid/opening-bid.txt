The IBM executive goes on to comment on AI chips after the US and the UK agreed to a $42 billion tech agreement and Big Tech continuing to spend heavily on artificial intelligence and building out AI infrastructures. Cohn also served as the director of the National Economic Council (NEC) under the first Trump administration, from 2017 to 2018.
