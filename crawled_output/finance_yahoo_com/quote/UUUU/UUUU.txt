Energy Fuels Inc. Overview Uranium / Energy
Energy Fuels Inc., together with its subsidiaries, engages in the exploration, recovery, recycling, exploration, operation, development, permitting, evaluation, and sale of uranium mineral properties in the United States. It produces and sells vanadium pentoxide, rare earth elements, carbonate, and heavy mineral sands, such as ilmenite, rutile, zircon, and monazite. The company was formerly known as Volcanic Metals Exploration Inc. and changed its name to Energy Fuels Inc. in May 2006. Energy Fuels Inc. was incorporated in 1987 and is headquartered in Lakewood, Colorado. www.energyfuels.com
