Super Group (SGHC) Limited Overview Gambling / Consumer Cyclical
Super Group (SGHC) Limited operates as an online sports betting and gaming operator. The company offers Betway, an online sports betting and casino offering; and Spin, a multi-brand online casino. It operates in Africa, the Middle East, the Asia-Pacific, Europe, North America, and South/Latin America. Super Group (SGHC) Limited is based in Saint Peter Port, Guernsey. www.sghc.com
