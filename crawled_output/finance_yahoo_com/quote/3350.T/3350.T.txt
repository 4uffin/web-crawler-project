Metaplanet Inc. Overview Restaurants / Consumer Cyclical
Metaplanet Inc. engages in hotel management operations in Japan. It operates through Hotel Business and Other segments. The company is also involved in various fields of Web3, blockchain, and NFT-related businesses; and real estate and investment services. In addition, it provides specialized consulting services to assist businesses in adopting Bitcoin. The company was incorporated in 1999 and is based in Tokyo, Japan. metaplanet.jp
