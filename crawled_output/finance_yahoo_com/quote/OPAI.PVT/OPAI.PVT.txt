OpenAI Overview
OpenAI is an AI research and deployment company whose mission is to ensure that artificial intelligence benefits all of humanity. Most known for its AI tool ChatGPT, the company promotes the development and deployment of AI systems that prioritize safety, fairness, and transparency. The organization conducts research in machine learning, deep learning, reinforcement learning, and related fields which spans a wide range of topics including natural language processing, computer vision, robotics, and the development of AI models.
